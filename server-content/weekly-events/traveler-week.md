# Traveler Week

Purpose: promote exploration and route reuse between Oakcross and nearby landmarks.

## Active window
- Weekly rotation slot: week 4
- Duration: 7 days

## NPC hooks
- Maren: traveler bulletin at notice board.
- Fen: route and waypoint guidance.
- Captain Rowan: safety advisory and return checkpoint.

## Objectives
- Visit at least one nearby landmark (ruin, tower, or forest edge).
- Return a route report to Fen or Rowan.
- Complete one exploration quest.

## Rewards
- Base: currency and exploration reputation.
- Bonus: temporary travel supply discount at tavern.

## Cleanup
- Disable traveler discounts.
- Reset bulletin board messages.
- Keep all content additive and reversible.
