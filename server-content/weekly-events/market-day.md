# Market Day

Purpose: drive player traffic through Oakcross market stalls and reward local trade loops.

## Active window
- Weekly rotation slot: week 1
- Duration: 7 days

## NPC hooks
- Maren: announces market schedule and bonus notice.
- Sella Reed: enables rotating market stock.
- Elira Thorn: offers tavern supply hand-in bonus.

## Objectives
- Complete 1 local delivery quest.
- Sell or trade 1 market bundle (seeds, crops, flowers, or food).
- Visit all 3 market-side points: tavern, stall, notice board.

## Rewards
- Base: small currency payout.
- Bonus: temporary discount token at market stall.

## Cleanup
- Disable bonus stock.
- Reset event board text to standard weekly loop.
- Keep all structures unchanged (additive only).
