# Gathering Week

Purpose: support farming and crafting loops with local collection objectives.

## Active window
- Weekly rotation slot: week 3
- Duration: 7 days

## NPC hooks
- Elira Thorn: food gathering hand-ins.
- Bram Coalhand: ore and fuel hand-ins.
- Sella Reed: market conversion offers.

## Objectives
- Deliver one farm bundle (wheat, carrots, or potatoes).
- Deliver one smithy bundle (raw iron and coal).
- Complete one gathering-oriented quest.

## Rewards
- Base: currency and town reputation.
- Bonus: temporary increased buy value for selected goods.
