# Oakcross Events and Reputation

## Reputation tiers
1. Outsider
2. Known Face
3. Trusted Hand
4. Town Friend

Unlocks by progression:
- Better shop inventory
- Additional dialogue branches
- Event-only rewards and discounts

## Weekly event loop
- Market Day
- Road Patrol
- Gathering Week
- Traveler Week

## Seasonal events mapped to Oakcross
- Spring (Bloomfair): farming, flowers, bees
- Summer (Sunreach): fishing, exploration, caravans
- Autumn (Harvestwake): crops, cooking, festival prep
- Winter (Frostwatch): survival supplies, danger routes
