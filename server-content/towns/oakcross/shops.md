# Oakcross Shops

Shops are physical and additive to existing builds.

## Smithy
- Core: tools, armor, repairs
- Progression: unlock better stock via ore/repair quests

## Tavern
- Core: food and travel supplies
- Seasonal: rotating meals and event-themed consumables

## Market Stall
- Core: seeds, flowers, utility items
- Event mode: rotating market-day and festival goods

Economy notes:
- Use EssentialsX + Vault-compatible pricing flow
- Gate premium offers behind reputation tiers
